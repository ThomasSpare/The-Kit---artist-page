THE KIT





THE UK DUO "THE KIT" a.k.a. Shawni Stark and Mike Hazard
is rapidly entering the Dance Music scene.
With full passion, the they work hard to bring new
tracks to the dancefloors.

Over the past two years, the duo has found their way into
their current style: a sweet combo of Minimal Techno and Synthwave.
their latest release 'Ascend' in collaboration with 'Dead Mouse'
reflects this much appreciated style very well.
